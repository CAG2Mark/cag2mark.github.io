Hello world; I'm Mark!
Since starting programming when I was 10, I have created web and software solutions for various organisations while still studying for high school. I also devote lots of time to my own personal projects, of which are free for use. It has have empowered individuals, generated careers and created brands.

I love playing with computers and bringing ideas to life. I enjoy fufilling others' needs with well-crafted software.

Currently, I am a student in Hong Kong studying for the IB Diploma.

Experience

I am fluent in web design and several programming languages, such as C#, Java, Python, JavaScript, TypeScript and more.

User experience is something I have a profound interest in - I tend to focus not only on the visual appeal, but also the ease of use.

Interests

Being a self-proclaimed nerd, I am hugely interested in maths and physics, and often apply my programming to solve problems in these areas of science.

In addition to this, I am also hugely interested in aviation, hence why many of my projects are software pertaining to flight simulators.
