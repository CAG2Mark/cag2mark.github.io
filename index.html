Made in: March 2017
At age: 13

FlyLive was the only tool of its kind available publicly at the time. It allowed flight simulator livestreamers to extract data from the flight simulator and display it on their stream through the use of constantly-updating text files and OBS.
FlyChrono

Made in: December 2017
At age: 14

FlyChrono was a time syncer for flight simulators. It would sync the simulator's time with the real-world time for extra realism. Other solutions existed but FlyChrono was the only free solution.

FlyLive Studio

Made in: December 2018
At age: 15

FlyLive Studio was both a breakthrough for my programming skill and the streaming community. It allowed people to have even richer overlays than before thanks to FlyLive Studio's built-in overlay editor. The nature of the overlay editor also provided much faster data update rates (up to 20 times a second, as opposed to once per second before). It also includes a chatbot built using the Twitch API.
